This interactive artwork was created in Shanghai in the spring of '22, when we were in the midst of an EPIDEMIC LOCKDOWN that was causing a lot of MENTAL STRESS to many of my collegues, friends, family, and myself. This sketch was designed to be a SIMPLE AND FUN COMPOSITION and MOVEMENT of circles, as well as an INTUITIVE WAY to ENGAGE and enjoy the magnificent dispersion effect.
